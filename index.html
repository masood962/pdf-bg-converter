How it works

Everything runs in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.

STEP 01

Upload

Select or drag a dark-background PDF. Your file stays entirely on your device.

STEP 02

Configure

Choose page range and toggle which elements to remove — logos, borders, dark backgrounds.

STEP 03

Convert

Each page is rendered and processed pixel-by-pixel using the Canvas API.

STEP 04

Download

Your clean, printer-friendly PDF is ready to download in seconds.

Frequently asked questions

Is my PDF uploaded to any server?
No. This tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device and no data is sent to any server.
What kind of PDFs does this work best on?
It works best on lecture slides and coaching notes (like Made Easy, Vision IAS, etc.) that have a dark or black background with white text. The algorithm detects dark regions and inverts them to white.
Why is it slow for large PDFs?
Each page is rendered at high resolution and processed pixel-by-pixel in the browser. A 70-page PDF may take 1–3 minutes. Keep the tab active during processing.
Can I process only some pages?
Yes. Use the "From page" and "To page" fields to specify a range. Pages outside the range are included in the output PDF but remain unchanged.
The logo is not fully removed — what can I do?
The logo removal uses a fixed region in the top-right corner. If the logo is in a different position, it may not be fully removed. We're working on auto-detection.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes, but processing may be slower on mobile devices. We recommend using a desktop or laptop for large PDFs.
